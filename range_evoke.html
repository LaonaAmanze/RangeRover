RANGE ROVER EVOQUE

Compact Luxury Meets Urban Sophistication

Performance & Powertrains

The Range Rover Evoque blends efficiency and responsiveness with modern hybrid powertrains, perfect for city life and weekend getaways.

Plug-in Hybrid (P300e)

  • 1.5L 3-cylinder petrol engine + electric motor
  • 227kW combined output
  • Up to 62km electric range
  • 0–100km/h in 6.4 seconds

Mild-Hybrid Petrol (P200 / P250)

  • 2.0L turbocharged engines with MHEV technology
  • Available in 147kW and 183kW
  • Refined driving with reduced emissions

Diesel Options (D165 / D200)

  • 2.0L mild-hybrid diesel engines
  • Great for long journeys and highway cruising

Interior & Technology

The Evoque offers a beautifully crafted interior with innovative technology, minimalist design, and sustainable luxury in every detail.

11.4" Curved Touchscreen

  • New floating-glass interface with Pivi Pro
  • All-in-one climate, navigation, and media control

Cabin Air Purification Plus

  • Air quality sensors and PM2.5 filtration
  • Promotes passenger health and comfort

Meridian™ Sound System

  • Exceptional sound quality with rich acoustics
  • Optional 3D surround for immersive experience

Sustainable Interior Materials

  • Kvadrat™ premium wool blend seat options
  • Soft-touch surfaces and tactile finishes
