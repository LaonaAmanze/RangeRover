The Range Rover line was launched in 1970 by British Leyland. It's known as a 4x4 luxury SUV. JLR builds Land Rovers in Brazil, China, India, Slovakia, and the United Kingdom. Land Rover, including the Range Rover brand, is owned by Jaguar Land Rover (JLR). JLR is a British multinational automobile manufacturer. JLR is a subsidiary of Tata Motors, an Indian automotive manufacturer.

History & Legacy 1948- The first Land Rover Series I was introduced, inspired by military jeeps.
1970- The iconic Range Rover was launched, combining luxury with off-road performance.
1989-Introduction of the Land Rover Discovery, making SUVs more family-friendly.
2008- Land Rover became part of Tata Motors after Ford sold the brand.
Present- Land Rover offers a lineup of high-tech, eco-friendly SUVs, including electric and hybrid models.
