DEFENDER 110

Built for Adventure, Refined for Comfort

Performance & Powertrains

The Defender 110 is engineered to conquer all terrains with powerful engine options and dynamic driving capability.

3.0L P400 MHEV Petrol

  • Inline-6 mild-hybrid petrol engine
  • 294kW power, 550Nm torque
  • 0–100km/h in 6.1 seconds

2.0L P300 Petrol

  • Turbocharged 4-cylinder engine
  • 221kW output
  • Fuel-efficient with ample off-road power

3.0L D300 Diesel MHEV

  • Strong torque output of 650Nm
  • Perfect for towing and long-distance journeys
  • Mild-hybrid tech enhances efficiency

Interior & Technology

Inside the Defender 110, rugged utility meets premium innovation — crafted for comfort during every expedition.

Intuitive Touchscreen Interface

  • 11.4" Pivi Pro curved glass display
  • Smartphone-like functionality

Sound & Connectivity

  • Meridian™ Sound System with surround clarity
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™

Utility Features

  • Durable rubber flooring with optional carpet inserts
  • Heated and cooled seats with memory
  • Configurable terrain response and electronic air suspension
