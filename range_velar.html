RANGE ROVER VELAR

Refined Elegance Meets Innovative Technology

Performance & Powertrains

The Range Rover Velar delivers a perfect balance of style and substance, offering a selection of refined engines with smooth, responsive power and hybrid efficiency.

Plug-in Hybrid (P400e)

  • 2.0L turbocharged petrol engine + electric motor
  • 297kW combined power output
  • 64km all-electric driving range
  • 0-100km/h in 5.4 seconds

Mild-Hybrid Petrol (P250 / P400)

  • 2.0L or 3.0L inline engines with MHEV technology
  • Available in 184kW (P250) and 294kW (P400) outputs
  • Excellent fuel efficiency and reduced emissions

Diesel Options (D200 / D300)

  • 2.0L and 3.0L diesel engines with MHEV
  • Ideal for long-distance and high-torque driving

Interior & Technology

Inside the Velar, you'll find a minimalist, high-tech cockpit with premium materials and state-of-the-art technology seamlessly integrated into its serene atmosphere.

Curved Touchscreen Display

  • 11.4" floating glass touchscreen
  • New curved interface with updated Pivi Pro OS

Meridian™ Sound System

  • 3D surround audio with precision detail
  • Immersive experience for all occupants

Cabin Air Purification Plus

  • Advanced air filtration system with CO₂ monitoring
  • Improves wellness and interior air quality

Luxury & Comfort

  • Heated, ventilated, and massaging seats
  • Sustainable leather-free textile options
  • Dual-zone climate control with touchscreen dials
