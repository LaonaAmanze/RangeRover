DEFENDER 110

Rugged Capability Meets Modern Utility

Performance & Powertrains

The Land Rover Defender 110 blends unstoppable off-road strength with refined on-road comfort and powerful engine choices for every kind of explorer.

Plug-in Hybrid (P400e)

  • 2.0L turbocharged petrol engine + electric motor
  • 297kW combined power
  • 43km all-electric range
  • 0-100km/h in 5.6 seconds

Diesel (D300)

  • 3.0L inline-six turbo diesel with mild hybrid assist
  • Delivers 221kW and strong torque for towing and trails

Petrol (P400)

  • 3.0L inline-six turbocharged petrol engine
  • Produces 294kW of power
  • 0-100km/h in 6.1 seconds


Interior & Technology

Inside the Defender 110, rugged durability meets modern luxury. Built for adventure, but equipped for everyday comfort.

Pivi Pro System

  • 11.4" central touchscreen
  • Integrated navigation and over-the-air updates

  • Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
  • Online pack with data plan and Wi-Fi hotspot

Versatile Seating

  • Up to 7 seats with flexible cargo options
  • Durable yet comfortable upholstery options

Adventure-Ready Features

  • Optional refrigerator compartment
  • Water-resistant rubber flooring with drain plugs
